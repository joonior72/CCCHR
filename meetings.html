Meetings

Dues are still $15.00 for all car clubs.

All car club member/representatives are invited to our meetings, which are scheduled the fourth Tuesday of every month; please check the calendar for details. We'll be discussing what and how the Car Club Counsel can help and represent the car clubs of the Southside of Hampton Roads and Northern North Carolina. Our goal is to provide a sounding board for all the car clubs on the Southside and to be a single voice in support of automotive legislative activities in Virginia and North Carolina. As well as a single location to advertise car club member events on our website calendar. We will also provide a central point for advertising of automotive related facilities and products.

Looking forward to seeing you all at the meeting!

Priority Chevrolet
1495 South Military Highway
Chesapeake, VA 23320
