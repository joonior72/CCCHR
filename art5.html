ARTICLE V: HONORARY MEMBERS

The Board may recognize the need to appointment Honorary Membership(s) to further develop CCCHR and its role in the community. Honorary members will be voted on by the Board, then by the general membership. Honorary Memberships will be for a period of one (1) year, and such membership(s) may be extended by a vote of the general membership. Honorary Members will have one (1) vote in all matters requiring a vote of the general membership.
