ARTICLE IV: TERMS OF OFFICE

BY RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD: The 2009 nominations for officers will be for VICE PRESIDENT and TREASURER to be installed in 2010, and the 2010 nominations for officers will be for PRESIDENT and SECRETARY to be installed in 2011. This will create a rotational election of officers to facilitate change with newly elected officers having the support of seated officers. This rotation of officers will continue unless a proposed alteration to the Bylaws reverts to all officers being elected at the same time.

Officers will be nominated in October of the year, with voting in November. Newly elected officers will be installed at the January meeting of the year(s) beginning their term(s).

Member club representatives who wish to serve on the board must have been a member for a period of twelve (12) months prior submitting their name(s) for consideration.

Member clubs can have no more than one (1) representative on the Board at any one time.
